## 🛸 About CrewCore

CrewCore loads as a **BepInEx 6 (IL2CPP) plugin** and adds a clean, tabbed in-game menu for **customizing private and custom matches** — custom modes, host/lobby controls, role and cosmetic options, plus a replay viewer. It's **community-driven**, updated regularly, and shaped by feedback from the Discord.

> 🎮 **For fun, with friends.** CrewCore is meant for private lobbies, custom games and consensual prank play among people who've opted in — not for disrupting public matches.
